Choose the challenge, not comfort

The road will be full of crossroads. There may be multiple choices, but everything boils down to a choice: stay in your comfort zone, or go outside your comfort zone.

There may be a moment in your life -- hopefully after decades of work -- when you will feel the need to cool down a bit because you will be satisfied with what you achieved. Until that moment, try to go out of your comfort zone. It will make you a better professional and you will feel more satisfied with your career. Remember that the best things often happen outside your comfort zone.
